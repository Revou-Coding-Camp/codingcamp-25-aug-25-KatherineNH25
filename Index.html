Vision N Mission

Vision: our vision is to create bold and stylish outfits that resonate with teenagers and fashion enthusiasts alike, offering pieces that are both versatile and universally admired. We aim to design clothing that inspires confidence, self-expression, and a sense of individuality for everyone who wears it.

Mission: Our mission is to share the story and meaning behind every collection, allowing each piece to carry a message of creativity, passion, and dedication. Through KATHRENE, we strive to showcase the patience, care, and artistry invested in every design, connecting with fashion lovers around the world and inviting them to experience the essence of our brand.



About

KATHREN is a contemporary fashion brand founded by designer Katherine, whose lifelong passion for style and self-expression inspired the creation of a label that blends boldness with elegance. Guided by a vision that merges feminine silhouettes with masculine strength, CARIX redefines modern fashion with pieces that are both daring and timeless. Rooted in creativity and individuality, the brand stands as a reflection of Katherine’s belief that clothing is more than fabric, it is identity, confidence, and art.
